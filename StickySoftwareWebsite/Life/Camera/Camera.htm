CamLink

    Take your HPC with you, and your digital camera suddenly
    becomes a lot more powerful. Now you can use your HPC to back
    up important images, or even send them by fax or email. Imagine
    sending back your holiday snaps while you are still on the beach!

    CamLink currently supports the Kodak DC20 and DC25 cameras.
    Although these cameras are no longer manufactured by Kodak,
    they can still be found. They are cheap, offer reasonable performance,
    and are ideal for use "in the field". DC20 owners like to strap their
    cameras to model airplanes and rockets, or use them as Webcams.

    Combining them with a HPC opens up all kinds of new facilities.
    Please note: this software uses a serial link with the camera. You
    will need to sort out a lead yourself between the HPC and the camera:
    it can be done using the existing leads, a null modem cable and
    various gender changers.

    Before you ask, yes: we would like to support other Digital cameras.
    If you have a favourite camera let us know, and let the camera
    manufacturer know too!
