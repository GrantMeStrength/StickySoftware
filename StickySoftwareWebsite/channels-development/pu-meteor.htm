Meteor Showers

Meteors can be seen throughout the year, but there are regular periods during which there are many more than usual. The following table lists these "showers" which are named after constellations from which they appear to radiate. The given date is when the shower reaches its busiest: there will be activity on days before and after this date. ZHR is the "Zenith Hourly Rate", an estimate as to how many meteors can be seen. The co-ordinates are where the meteors will appear to come from in the sky.
