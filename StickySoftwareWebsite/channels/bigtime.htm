Big Time

The first of a new kind of Palmsize PC utility

Looking for an easy to use clock for your Palmsize PC? Tired of squinting at the time in the taskbar? Then Big Time is for you. With ultra-large digits, your Palmsize PC is transformed into a useful clock. Keep it in its cradle on your desktop, beside your bed or just take it out of your pocket and use as a watch.

Big Time is a new type of Palmsize PC utility: it's a Mobile Desktop Component. Effectively, it becomes part of your Active Desktop, with all the flexibility that implies. You decide where on the screen it appears, and you get to select the other objects on the desktop and how they will fit around it.

For more details on Installing the Using Mobile Components, follow this link!

With digits which are large enough to read from a distance, Big Time is going to be an indispensable addition to your Palmsize PC. And there's one final trick: click on the Sticky Software banner, and you'll launch the Calendar application – a handy shortcut.

Another first from Sticky Software, and yet another free download from your favourite Windows CE software developer. Please use the Update Now feature of your IE4 explorer if you haven't download the latest version of Big Time. There will be new graphics and other features added, and you can stay up to date with only one click from your desktop!
