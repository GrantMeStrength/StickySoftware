To install Sticky Buttons, please perform the following steps:

1. Make sure your Palmsize PC's AutoSync options include the synchronising of Mobile Channels.

2. Click on the button below to copy the Sticky Buttons Mobile Desktop Component to your PC.
You must be using IE4 or later!

3. Click on the third option: "Yes, Notify me of updates.. etc etc"

You may see error messages in IE4 -- this is normal! The IE4 Web browser will by
default try to execute the channel. As the channel contains information for the PalmPC
only, it will fail. You can therefore ignore most errors.

Sometimes IE4 seems to crash when the channel is selected. This may or may not
be normal. It is however, irritating. If you restart IE4 and select the Channels from the
tool strip at the top of the display, you should see your Mobile Channel listed. Right-click
and select Update Now and the information should be copied across.

3. Synchronise your Palmsize PC with your Desktop PC.

4. On the Palmsize PC, open the Start button, then Settings and then Display.
Select the Active Desktop setting, and you should see the new entries in the list.
Select it.

5. When you click OK and return to your Palmsize PC desktop, the new components will be present.
