On this day...

Important - and not so important - events from the past

Almost every day as you switch on your PalmPC, you will be given an exciting event with which to bore your friends and enemies.

OnThisDay... is a Mobile Desktop Component. Effectively, it becomes part of your Active Desktop, with all the flexibility that implies. You decide where on the screen it appears, and you get to select the other objects on the desktop and how they will fit around it.
