Some Frequently Asked Questions

 

Q. Does this work now?

A. Yes! There was a problem with the MFC DLL file in a previous version. This has been fixed.

Q. Do I have to tap the on-screen buttons to control it?

A. No, you can use the jog wheel to rotate left/right, Action button to more forward and Escape button to fire. It's recommended you switch off the button sound effects. It's a good idea to turn off these sound effects anyway, as they are irritating.

Q. Can I download it directly to my Jornada Palmsize PC?

A. Well, yes in theory you can, but you can't install it or use it if you do. Download it to your desktop PC and run it from there.

Q. Will it work on a different PsPC? For example, my Compaq Aero/E-100/Nino 500?

A. No, it will only work on the Jornada 420.

Q. Will it work on my handheld PC?

A. Nope. There are other versions for these models elsewhere on the Sticky Software website.

Q. So let me get this right, it's free, yeah?

A. No! It's not free. It's charity-ware. If you play it, you are under a moral obligation to make a charitible donation.

Q. In the pictures above, the taskbar at the bottom of the screen is missing. Howd'ya do that?

A. Go to Start/Settings/Taskbar and click on Auto Hide.
