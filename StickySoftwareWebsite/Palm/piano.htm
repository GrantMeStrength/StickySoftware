PalmPiano
 
Here's the first "musical" instrument for the PalmsizePC,
a single octave keyboard for you to tap out your favourite
tunes.

You can even use the simple sequencer to record your
best works, and replay them later to the adoration of your
friends. Just don't hold us responsible for the results...

 
 

PalmPiano for the PalmsizePC is free. All we ask is that
you do not store the file on another web site or place it
on CD-ROM -- if you want to provide a link, then please
link to www.sticky.net.Thank you.

Download PalmPiano for Palmsize PCs

 

 

This file is a self-extracting archive. Connect your PsPC
to your desktop machine and double-click it. PalmPiano
will be installed in the Start/Programs/ directory
on your palmsize computer. Please report any problems
to support@sticky.net , thank you.

This file requires 300K of memory, and currently is not
suitable for Compact Flash installtion (although this
will be supported in the future). Please note that some
PsPCs will automatically shut down when playing
sounds if battery life is low.
