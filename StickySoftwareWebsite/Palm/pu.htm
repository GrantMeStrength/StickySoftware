Maybe you like the warmth of being inside, but would still like to watch the Moon as it changes phase, or watch as Orion rises and moves across the sky?

Try Pocket Universe and you'll be amazed at how much your tiny little handheld computer can do. It's the ideal companion for amateurs and beginners, and will help you find your way about the sky.

Features

  • Online help (click START and then HELP)
  • Easy to use, standard installation routine
  • Plots the positions of the Sun, Moon and Planets
  • Not a static map - a unique dynamic view!
  • Plots the positions of almost 5000 stars
  • Choose to view only the brighter stars
  • Instant Goto to find a planet or particular location in the sky
  • Works from any position and timezone on Earth
  • EPOCH 2000 star co-ordinates
  • Powerful built-in "time machine"
  • Auto-lock to object: watch as the sun rises and sets
  • Invert the screen colours to make viewing easier
  • Tap the display to instantly goto that location
  • Variable Zoom in and out, with full sky view
  • Many display elements may be toggled on and off
  • Outlines for major constellations, includng the zodiac.
  • Names of brightest stars
  • Sun and Moon rendered at "actual size".
  • Lunar phases
  • Messier objects
  • Location of Jovian moons
  • Save and Load settings, for different locations or preferences
  • Floating toolbar for quickly viewing N,S,E,W and zooms
  • Installation by NStall

Demo Version

 Please feel free to download this demonstration version of Pocket Universe for the Palmsize PC. Note that this program requires approx 400K of memory free on the PsPC, although it can be stored and run from a CF card. We use Viking cards from Shore Systems.

This demo version is not time limited, but has a reduced number of features. It does not contain a full star catalog, nor on-line help.
