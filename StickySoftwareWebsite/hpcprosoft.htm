Available Now! PowerView v2.1

A very useful way to keep an eye on how much battery power your HPC Pro has remaining. A tooltray icon keeps you up to date with a glance, while a dialog provides you with in-depth information. Don't get caught short again!

Available NOW for the Vadem Clio/Sharp Tripad, and HP Jornada 820.

Download the file, copy it to your HPC Pro and double-click to start it. An icon appears in the tooltray to keep you up-to-date with battery power level. Tap the icon, and the graph window will open.

This is a FREE utility, there are no nag screens or license fees to pay.

Please select your HPC Pro model:

  • PowerView for HP Jornada
  • PowerView for Vadem Clio/Sharp Tripad

Note: if the graph dialog is kept open for extended periods, the Jornada HPC Pro may pop-up a warning message. Simple cancel it to continue.
