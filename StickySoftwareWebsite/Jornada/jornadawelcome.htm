May Update

PowerToys and PlusPack

Run, don't walk, to the Microsoft web site and download these software archives immediately. At a stroke, Microsoft have changed the way you'll use your HP820 forever. Things just got a lot easier, and many niggles with CE have been fixed into the bargain.

Need a wordcount for Pocket Word? No problem. Want to spellcheck your email? Consider it done. How do you create and view images? Easy: the Paint and Image-viewer programs. Want to create a PowerPoint presentation on the road? Now you can use Pocket Word.

This is only the start, with more sound schemes, Infrared and networking utilities... it's like getting a whole new computer! You'll find the links on the Compatible Software page.

CE on Show

I'm just back from a few days at a Windows show in Dublin, where I was helping out at the Windows CE stand. HP were also there (via a reseller) and had the entire range on display. As well as the 820 and dinky 420, I got a chance to play with the new 680 and what a lovely piece of hardware it is. With its smaller than 620 size and yet real keyboard, this is the best example of why a handheld PC running Windows CE beats all rivals. I would seriously consider buying it as well as the 820: one for the pocket, one for the plane, train or office.
